Utopian for investors

On of the main challenges of developing an Open Source project is often the lack of funding for functions the project owner cannot fill on their own. For investors, it is difficult to choose which projects to support, or predict any kind of return on their investment.

We are here to change that!

Utopian is the only platform rewarding contributions to Open Source projects by utilizing a decentralized, vote-based reward system built on top of the STEEM blockchain. Utopian is your chance to support the Open Source community and receive a positive ROI.
